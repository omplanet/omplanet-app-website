Our Vision

In the midst of this accelerated time of changes, many collectives and individuals from all over the world are stepping forward as radiant examples of a new way of living, new society and a New Earth.

When we have first discovered these collectives and individuals, it has sparked a great inspiration to connect them, to help them thrive and shine for the world and it is now expressed as this platform.

We are not a business that measures its success based on how much profit it makes. We don't put profit above serving our users.

Empowering rather than Distracting

We do not treat you as an addicted Facebook user by suggesting you attractive content for endless scrolling to keep you using our app and waste your precious time for minimal satisfaction.

We want you to find what might bring you joy in real life as soon as possible and to go spend time on that real life activity and real human interaction.

We highlight places and experiences that are transformational, that bring people together, or empower people to find warmth and loving support just a click away.

Each community is offered its own dedicated environment where all its members will be connected. OMPlanet users can manage their membership subscriptions, interact with other members, create and share content within their communities or between all other communities.

Protecting Privacy

We respect your privacy and do not spy, track, rent or sell your data to other parties. You are in control of how your data is being shared and you are the owner of your personal information and content. Read the Privacy Policy for more details.
