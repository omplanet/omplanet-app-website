OMPlanet

Intentional Social Networking App for Conscious Communities

Welcome to OMPlanet - a space where everything comes together to assist your journey of self-realization, consciousness expansion and creativity, where you may pursue your truest form of PLAY in a collective of like-minded and like-open-hearted people.

Set your intention to live in unity, with purpose and service to others, to share abundantly joy, love and friendship. We give you the tools to make magic happen!

- Our primary services for individuals are to connect with like-minded people, to discover transformational places and experiences: all accessible with the basic membership plan.

- Our primary service for organizations and communities is to have a dedicated platform for interaction for all the members of the collective, to have their own branded version of the app to efficiently communicate and exchange services. If you are an intentional community, positive impact businesse, NGO or a conscious movement: you have come to the right place!

Organize and Grow Your Community

There are times when the power of many as One is required for us to create a significant change during this planetary transition! Become an inspirational example how to bring people together around shared purpose and mission.

OMPlanet app is designed to bring together and enliven collectives of people like intentional communities, positive change businesses, integrative healing centers, educational organizations and conscious movements. Members of each collective can build meaningful relationships with each other, efficiently communicate and exchange services on a regular basis.

As a community builder you can request to have your own version of the OMPlanet app with a dedicated interaction space only for your collective. You can manage membership subscriptions, create and share news and promote activities.

FAQ's

How can I support this project?

We welcome co-creators who share our vision! There are specific skills that are most needed at this moment - mobile developers, UI/UX designers, financial specialists, marketing, communication, content management and customer support. Feel free to contact us if any of these is exciting for you to work with.

Will the apps be free?

The apps will be free to install. You can have free access to explore the main OMPlanet community. In order to have access to specific communities, there will be a membership fee for each community.

Who’s funding OMPlanet?

The initial stages of the OMPlanet development were funded by the personal investment of its core founders. In order to be financially independent, we will receive funds for the growth and scaling of the project from our member communities through their membership subscriptions. We are also open to receive investments from aligned investors who share the vision and the values of OMPlanet. We will NOT be accepting money from investors whose exclusive interest is ROI, maximising profit over doing good!

Which devices is OMPlanet available on?

There are native Android and iOS apps available on the app stores and a web interface with a limited access for community moderators.

New Earth Directory Connecting All

In many corners of the world there are thousands of initiatives, conscious movements or spiritual communities where people come together to co-create and transform, to celebrate and share their gifts.

In 2008, through some interesting synchronicities, we have come together with 3 friends from Armenia, Russia and Germany to initiate a new project called "The City of 12". It was a dream project to build - "A New City for New Humans to grow in Truth, Love and Freedom". At that time we did not have enough resources and readiness of the collective, our dream project did not come to fruition. Although over the years we have witnessed the emergence of similar initiatives in other places of the world. And then there was a spark of a great inspiration and intention to connect them all!

That intention has been transformed and evolved many times in the past 10 years, and now it is expressed as this platform, this is a co-creation in progress, a quest.

“Be the Change You Want to See

OUR PRINCIPLES

  • Unity

    We recognize that we are all inherently connected, we are all one interdependent, interconnected Unity.

  • Connect From Heart to Hart

    We want to act from an authentic, loving space in our encounters and to see the creative heart at play in the aspirations of others.

  • Support Personal Growth and Collective Evolution

    We intend to serve the conscious evolution of humanity, the highest good for all, a revolution beyond social activism. We don’t fight against old patterns, we instead recognize new ones appearing everywhere as individuals and collectives learn how beautiful human nature can be.

  • Conscious Business

    We are not a business, measuring success by profit. Creating collective abundance through win-win interactions or exchanges is a virtuous cycle. There is much to be done with what we may earn and we will never compromise our vision and principles for personal gain. And we simply love creating and exchanging our gifts!

  • Striving for High Quality and Integrity

    We honour one another’s liberty, sovereignty, autonomy, independence, individuality and self-responsibility.

  • Empowering rather than Distracting

    Humanity embraces technology but we can also become slaves to it. We will use technology to help us all find joy in real life and authentic human interaction.

    We do not treat you as an addicted Facebook user by suggesting you attractive content for endless scrolling to keep you using our apps and waste your precious time for minimal satisfaction.

    We highlight places and experiences that are meaningful and transformational, that bring people together, that empower people to share warmth and loving support in real life.

  • Protecting Your Privacy

    We respect your privacy and do not spy, track or sell your data to other parties. You are in control of how your data is being shared. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

  • Giving You the Ownership of your Data

    You are the owner of your personal information and content. You will be free to remove your data whenever you like.
